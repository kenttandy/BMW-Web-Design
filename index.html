A Legendary German Automaker

Bayerische Motoren Werke

  • Bayerische Motoren Werke AG (BMW) is a German automobile manufacturer known for luxury and performance.
  • Founded in 1916 in Munich, BMW started as an aircraft engine producer before moving to motorcycles and cars.
  • Key milestones include the R32 motorcycle (1923) and the 3/15 automobile (1929), along with iconic models like the M series.
  • BMW is recognized for innovation, including lightweight designs and advanced driver assistance systems, embodying the slogan "The Ultimate Driving Machine."
  • The company emphasizes sustainability with electric and hybrid vehicles, notably the i Series (i3 and i8).
  • BMW has production facilities in Germany, the U.S., China, and South Africa, supported by a global dealership network.
  • With a focus on innovation and sustainability, BMW continues to lead in the automotive industry.
